Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my professional headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that matches your industry standards. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide a detailed styling guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Individual sessions usually last 30-45 minutes, including time for wardrobe changes and different poses. Group sessions are scheduled based on team size, with minimal wait time for each person.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive both high-resolution and web-optimized images, perfect for print and digital use. Each photo comes with professional retouching and multiple cropping options.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional makeup artists and hair stylists who can be booked for your session. This service should be arranged at least 48 hours before your shoot.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand that plans change. You can reschedule your session with 48 hours notice at no additional charge. Last-minute changes may incur a rebooking fee.
